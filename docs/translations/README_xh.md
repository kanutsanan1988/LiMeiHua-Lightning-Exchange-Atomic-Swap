Kuba imfazwe yorhwebo kwaye urhwebo yimfazwe. Imbali iyasixelela ngaloo nto. Xa kungekho nzuzo, akukho sizathu sokuba nabani na afune ukulwa imfazwe kwakhona.

Imfazwe yokushicilela imali.
ISebe lezeMali likhupha iibhondi zemfazwe, libhengeza ukuba abantu mazithenge. Ukuba akukho mntu uzithengayo, ibhanki enkulu iya kuzithenga ngokwayo. Nantsi indlela ukuprintwa kwemali kuqala ngayo.
Imali iya kusasazeka kwi-supply chain yoshishino lwemfazwe, kwiinkampani ezahlukeneyo eMelika ngokwayo, kunye nemali enokuthengisa izixhobo kuwo onke amazwe ehlabathi ngexesha lemfazwe, elikwayimali eninzi kakhulu.
Emva koko, xa imali yenkampani yezixhobo ingena kwinkqubo yebhanki yorhwebo, ubungakanani bemali kwinkqubo buya kwanda nge-10-20 amaxesha e-United States (eThailand, imalunga ne-6-7 amaxesha). Ngokukhutshwa kwemali-mboleko ngokuphindaphindiweyo ngemali efanayo (ukuprintwa kwemali okanye ukwanda kobungakanani bemali kwinkqubo yezoqoqosho eyenzeka kwinqanaba lebhanki yorhwebo, iFractional Reserve Banking) ingena kwinkqubo yezoqoqosho yaseMelika njengendawo yokuqala yomcimbi weCantillon effect yenkqubo yezemali yehlabathi ukusuka kwindawo ephambili yokushicilela yehlabathi, iUnited States.
Oku kukhuthaza uqoqosho ngezizathu zemfazwe ngoshishino lwemfazwe. Ukuprintwa kwemali ngakunye kwindawo yokushicilela imali kukuhluthwa kwamandla okuthenga okanye ukuhluthwa kwexabiso lemali kubo bonke abantu emhlabeni jikelele. Ukuhluthwa kwelizwe ngalinye. Okukhona ilizwe linezixa zemali ezigciniweyo eziphezulu zeedola, kokukhona lihluthwa ngakumbi. Abakhathali nokuba bayayiphumelela okanye bayayilahlekelwa yimfazwe. Bakhathalele kuphela ukuba bangaprinta malini ukuhlutha abantu behlabathi. (Enye into, xa kukho ingxaki yezoqoqosho, iinkampani ezahlukeneyo emhlabeni jikelele ziya kunciphisa iindleko ngokugxotha abasebenzi kwaye zibathathele indawo nge-AI exabisa kancinci, kodwa wonke umntu emhlabeni kufuneka athenge amandla okucubungula e-AI evela eMelika ngoku.)
